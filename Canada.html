C$ 5

←Observe

The note has a frosted maple leaf window, a portrait of Sir Wilfrid Laurier, and a hologram strip with Laurier’s portrait, the Mackenzie Tower, and repeated denominations. The strip also features a maple leaf border. The denomination and issue year are on the right

Reverse→

This side of the note features a space innovation theme. It includes the denomination, a mirrored hologram strip, images of Canadarm2, Dextre, and an astronaut with a Canadian flag patch. Below is Earth from space, showing the Great Lakes, Hudson Bay, and the Gulf of St. Lawrence. Five stylized stars represent the denomination, and the reverse side of the maple leaf window is on the right
C$ 5

The left side of the note shows the issuing institute, Canada’s coat of arms, and a holographic stripe. It features Sir Wilfrid Laurier’s portrait, the West Block of Parliament, the face value “5,” a watermark of Laurier, and a Canadian flag. The note’s colors are mainly blue and yellow

Reverse→

The image shows Canadian winter sports: children playing hockey, a boy tobogganing, and a child learning to ice skate with an adult. In the middle is a bilingual quote
