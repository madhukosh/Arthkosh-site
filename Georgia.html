anc 10

←Observe

The main image of the obverse of the banknote consists of an image of a Caucasian leopard (Panthera pardus tulliana) and an ornamented ancient bronze axe. In the right and left sectors there are images of the state emblem of the Republic of Abkhazia and the denomination of the banknote

Reverse→

The main image of the reverse side of the banknote consists of images of a bronze ax and the upper part of the traditional Abkhaz military and hierarchical staff “alabasha”. The field on the reverse side also shows the denomination and serial number of the banknote
