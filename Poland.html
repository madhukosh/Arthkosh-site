zł 50

←Observe

The front of the banknote features a portrait of Karol Świerczewski - a polish general of the Red Army. To the right of the portrait - White Eagle without the crown - coat of arms of the People's Republic of Poland

Reverse→

On the reverse side is an Order Krzyża Grunwaldu (Order of the Cross of Grunwald) - a military award
zł 100

←Observe

The front of the banknote features a portrait of Ludwik Waryński - editor of the magazine “Proletaryat" To the right of the portrait - White Eagle without the crown - coat of arms of the People's Republic of Poland

Reverse→

Against the background of flying banners, a vignette of the first issue of the magazine “Proletaryat”
zł 500

←Observe

Front: portrait of Tadeusz Kościuszko, Supreme Commander during the Kościuszko Uprising. Right: White Eagle without crown, coat of arms

Reverse→

Banner of Scythmen (Kosciuszko Insurgents); inscription “ŻYWIĄ Y BRONIĄ” (They Feed and Defend). Sheaf of grain, laurel wreath, crossed scythe, and pike
