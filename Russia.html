₽ 1

←Observe

The banknote features a pale yellow-green security underprint with waving lines. The USSR coat of arms is in the upper left corner, above a cartouche with an inscription. The denomination is in the center, with the date below and a red serial number to the right. A pale yellow cartouche with the digit "1" is on the right white panel. The watermark consists of rows of light and dark 5-pointed stars. Does this work for you?

Reverse→

The left side has a small white panel with a red-yellow digit “1” at the bottom. The right side features a large panel with a pale yellow-green security underprint and a multicolored cartouche with a yellow-green digit “1” and floral elements. The denomination is printed in red at the center and repeated in 14 languages in a white cartouche below
